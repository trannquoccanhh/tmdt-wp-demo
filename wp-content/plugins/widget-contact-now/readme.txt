== Description ==

**Contact Info**

The Contact Now widget is a quick way to create contact information through the contact widget on wordpress admin, without touching a single line of code.
This plugin shows your contact info with icons. Add the widget to a widget position like the footer or sidebar, fill in the info, 
choose an icon color and press save. Your contact info with icons is now being displayed on the front-end of your website!

**Features**

Add contact information quickly and easily with ready-made labels, unlimited color customization.

Display gorgeous contact information on your website with simple, easy-to-use widgets.

**Contact Information**

Display your contact information including company name, work address, telephone number, mobile number, email address, fax number, opening and closing hours, and calendar.

[Plugin Homepage](https://longvietweb.com/plugins/widget-contact)

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire 'widget-contacts-now' folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Use the Appearance->Widget Drag the Contacts Information widget to the footer or sidebar or widget-are you want it to appear. screen to configure the plugin.
